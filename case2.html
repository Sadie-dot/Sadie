Preserving a client relationship with proactive communication as a critical bug risked disaster

A failed launch could shutter client. App had a fatal flaw — now what?

  • We had to maintain trust despite a showstopper bug discovered before user acceptance testing (UAT).
  • Not delivering in time would jeopardize the success of client's company, including their financial backing.
  • I liaised with our engineers & the client to keep stakeholders informed of developments until solved.

My job was to ensure deliverables matched needs. The stakes were high for everyone.

👤

My role: Client services activities, including relationship management, advocating for client, and problem-solving with internal & external stakeholders.

The team: R&D & delivery engineers to ID issue, check specifications with technical limitations, and review code.

My goal was to use open, proactive communication to reassure client of our commitment to a solution.

  • Anticipated questions so client not left waiting.
  • Shared what we knew & what we did not yet know.
